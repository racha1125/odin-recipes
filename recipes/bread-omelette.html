Bread Omelette

Description

Omelette is one the tastiest and easiest breakfast you can make on a busy morning. Pair it with bread and your wholesome meal is ready.

Ingredients

  • 2 egg
  • 4 slices brown bread
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • salt as required
  • 2 teaspoon finely chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped green chilli
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion

Directons

  1. Take the bread slices and remove the centre with the help of a bowl.
  2. Now take another bowl and beat eggs in it using an electric beater.
  3. After beating the eggs, add green chillies, onion, coriander leaves and salt to the eggs, whisk well.
  4. Now, put a non-stick pan over medium flame. Place the centre chopped bread in the pan and put half of the egg mixture in the centre of the bread.
  5. Then, cover it with another bread slice. Cook for 1-2 minutes on one side and then flip to cook the other side as well for 2 minutes.
  6. Repeat the step to use the leftover mixture and bread. Once done, serve hot and crisp with butter on the top.
